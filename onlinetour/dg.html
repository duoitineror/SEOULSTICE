Daegu is a beautiful city located in the southeastern part of South Korea. Known for its rich culture, fashion, and traditional markets, Daegu offers both modern city life and historical charm. Visitors can explore Seomun Market, relax at Apsan Park, or enjoy the Daegu Chimac Festival (chicken and beer festival) held every summer.

Fun Fact: Daegu is also called the "City of Apple Blossoms" because of its sweet apples grown in the region!
