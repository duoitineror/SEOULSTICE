Busan

Busan is South Korea’s coastal playground, where golden beaches and fresh seafood define the lifestyle.

Natural Attractions

Haeundae Beach

Famous beach with luxury hotels and vibrant athmosphere

Taejongdae

Natural park with stunning sea cliffs and panomaric views

Songdo Beach

Scenic beach with the Songdo Cloud Trails skywalk.

Cultural & Historical Sites

Gamcheon Culture Village

Famous beach with luxury hotels and vibrant athmosphere

Haedong Yonggungsa

Perched dramatically on a cliff overlooking the East Sea, this Buddhist temple is as spiritual as it is scenic. Sunrise here is breathtaking—a blend of nature and faith you won’t forget.

Beomeosa Temple

Historic mountain temple in a tranquil setting.

Markets & Shopping

  • Jagalchi Market: Korea’s largest seafood market.
  • Nampo-dong: Bustling shopping district with street vendors and eateries.

Festivals & Events

  • Busan International Film Festival (BIFF): Annual event showcasing international films.

Outdoor Activities

  • Hiking: Trails in Geumjeongsan and around Pusan National University.
  • Dongbaekseom: Picturesque island with walking paths and the Nurimaru APEC House.
