Booking

3 day package

  • -Day 1: Climbing Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak), and exploring the seafood markets of the Seongsan area.
  • -Day 2: Hike a Hallasan trail (choose trail by fitness level) or visit Jeju’s waterfalls & basalt cliffs near Seogwipo.
  • -Day 3: Explore coastal cafés, Jeju Stone Park, or beach time. Rent a car for flexibility (public transit is slower between sights). Many travelers drive around the island to maximize time.
