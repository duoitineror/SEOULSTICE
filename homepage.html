Welcome to South Korea!

South Korea is special for its dynamic mix of advanced technology and deep-rooted traditions, showcased through its globally influential K-pop and drama scene (Hallyu), innovative electronics and automotive industries, unique cuisine, and world-leading internet infrastructure. Its modern cities blend futuristic architecture with ancient palaces, while its cultural heritage is preserved in UNESCO sites and vibrant traditional markets.

Popular places include..

Gyeongbokgung Palace
Myeong-dong shopping district
Haeundae Yonggungsa
