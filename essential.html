🇰🇷 South Korea Travel Guide

Language Basics

  • Annyeong (안녕) – Hello/Goodbye (informal)
  • Kamsahamnida (감사합니다) – Thank you (formal)
  • Juseyo (주세요) – Please (request)
  • Mianhamnida (미안합니다) – Sorry (formal)

Transportation

  • Subway: Reliable, easy to navigate. Use the T-money card for convenience.
  • KTX: High-speed train connecting major cities.
  • T-money Card: Prepaid card for transit, taxis, and convenience stores.

Currency & Budget Tips

  • Currency: South Korean Won (KRW).
  • Budget: Affordable to mid-range; luxury options available. Street food is a great budget-friendly option.
  • ATMs: Widely available. Credit cards are accepted in most places.

Safety & Etiquette

  • South Korea is very safe. Just be cautious in crowded areas.
  • Etiquette: Respect for elders and formal behavior is important in both public and private settings.

Travel Styles

  • Solo Travel: Safe and easy, with a lot to explore.
  • Family: Kid-friendly attractions and activities.
  • Backpacking: Budget-friendly accommodations and vibrant social scenes.
  • Luxury: High-end hotels, spas, and fine dining experiences.
